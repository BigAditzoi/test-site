Then we arrived in Javea

After spending 4 months in Rousillon, in France and suffering a few disappointments we decided to head down the Mediterean coast towards Valencia. That was part of our plan B. If France wouldn't work, we would then try:

  1. Spain
  2. Italy
  3. Portugal
  4. Greece
  5. and, finally, Romania

During a small interlude in Barcelona with some buddies from my last place of work, which happened to flew in from Luxemburg, where they were on assignment, one of them suggested to venture a stop in Javea, where he had spent a vacation with his family a few years back.

We weren't too sure, especially after we arrived in Valencia and found a listing for a penthouse in a very nice area at a reasonable price.
